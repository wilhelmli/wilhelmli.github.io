Haojiang Li 李镐江

This is a Chinese name; the family name is Li.

Haojiang Li (Chinese: 李镐江; pinyin: Lǐ Hàojiāng, English pronunciation; born July 28, 1997), alias Wilhelm, is a Chinese industrial designer based in Vancouver, BC Canada. Li is known for his project HALT CREATION, an outlet of futuristic design ideas. In December 2019, Li graduated with a BA in Economics from University of Victoria, the class of 2020.

Currently, Li is residing in Guangdong, China working on multiple projects while preparing a portfolio for Masters applications.
