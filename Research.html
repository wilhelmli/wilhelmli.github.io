Haojiang Li (Chinese: 李镐江; pinyin: Lǐ Hàojiāng), alias Wilhelm. I graduated with a BA from the University of Victoria.

My research interests are in urban land economics, applied intelligence, and industrial design. My current work focuses on contemporary economic development, and operations management.

I study how user experience of technologies and technological advancements shape the modern business scene. I am also interested in how technologies empower SMBs, and how better international policy can support economic development and well-being.
