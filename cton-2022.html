Exhibition Profile

电矩狂魔 DianJuKuangMo (CTON) is a car themed interactive art show organized by Li in 2022. It summaries all projects of CTON in the years of 2021 and 2022. It represents a series of deep introspections on the revolution of electric vehicles in modern times.
